Question 2

How many spells does a 20th Level Cleric have prepared (assuming no magic items and 20 Wisdom)

A. 20

B. 35

C. 25

D. 30

CORRECT!!

Clerics have 20 spells prepared for being level 20, an extra 5 spells prepared for having 20 Wisdom, and an extra 10 spells from their subclass domain
